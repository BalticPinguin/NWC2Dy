Welcome to the documentation page of NW2Dy

Introduction

The programme is called with three arguments:
  • final_file.nwo
  • initial_file.nwo
  • outfile
where final_file.nwo and initial_file.nwo are log-files of a modified nwchem-programme. The outfile is used for further analysis and the PES-calculation.

Requirements to nwchem-Calculations

besides having the respective patches applied, the programme expects certain calculations of Nwchem to be done:
  • unrestricted open-shell (td)dft calculation:

    DFT

    odft
